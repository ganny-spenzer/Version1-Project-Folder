Asus ROG Phone 6 pro 5G Details

Asus ROG Phone 6 Pro is a gaming smartphone loaded with inciting specs and features like a robust Snapdragon 8+ Gen1 chipset, a big 6,000mAh battery supported by a 65W fast charger, a 6.7-inch touchscreen AMOLED panel with 165Hz refresh rate, and it has got a 50MP triple-rear & a 12MP front camera. Let’s read more about the smartphone in detail and see what more it has to offer.


Asus ROG Phone 6 Pro Display and Camera

Asus ROG Phone 6 Pro smartphone is fitted with a 6.7-inch AMOLED touchscreen display. It comes with an FHD+ resolution of 1080x2448px with a pixel density of 395ppi and up to 800-nits (typ) of brightness. Also, the panel refreshes at 165Hz and has an HDR10+ feature for better viewing. Besides, A layer of Gorilla Glass Victus protects the display from scratches and minor falls.

A vertically aligned 50MP triple camera is placed on the backside of the mobile. It consists of a 50MP primary camera (Sony IMX766 sensor), a 13MP ultrawide camera, and a 5MP macro camera with autofocus. The phone offers a 12MP front camera for assisting with selfies and video calls. Moreover, the main rear camera can record UHD 8K videos @24fps
